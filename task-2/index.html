Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to book an appointment?

No referral is required. You can book directly with any OrthoNow specialist online or by phone.

Do you accept health insurance?

We accept most major health insurance providers. Call your nearest clinic to confirm coverage before your visit.

Is parking available at your clinics?

Yes, all OrthoNow clinics offer on-site or nearby patient parking.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, any previous scans or reports, and a list of current medications.

Can I get a same-day appointment for a sports injury?

Same-week appointments are available at all clinics, and several locations offer same-day slots for urgent injuries. Call ahead to check availability.
